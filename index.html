KNOW ABOUT US

Building a Sustainable and Compassionate Society

Savaj Education and Charitable Trust is a non governmental organisation established in 2020 in Gir-Somnath Gujarat, India.

Trust is run by students and we creating sustainable and humane society where in all living being have access to manage their livelihood.

Savaj Trust is focused in orphanage support, child rights, education support, old-age support, woman empowerment, medical support, care of birds and animals in Gujarat.
